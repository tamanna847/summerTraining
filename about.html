ABOUT

at our sweet centre we offer a wide range of farsan,tempting sweets and other delicases.we also use only the best quality ingredients in the preparation of our product,and each ingredients is sourced and imported from the finest in the world.our flour,almonds,pistachios, cashews,khajur,figs,raisins and saffron come to you directly from source.even the ghee and sugar,essential ingredients in sweet making,are of premium quality.

we use high quality raw ingredients to prepare these farsan and sweets. our team of skilled professionals engage in extensive research to prepare these colourful and eye catching sweets with delicious taste for the sweet-tooth- connoisseurs.ingredients that we use for the preparation also vary from flour,to milk and dry fruits.we also provide custom orders to meet your needs.
